Based on Goldilocks and the Three Bears, a 19th-century English fairy tale. Goldilocks, a young little girl, who lives in the village with her parents, wandered away into the forest and came across a house. She entered the house and discovered three bowls in the kitchen and decided to pick the small bowl. She then walks to the living room where she discovers three chairs…. big, medium, and small, she decides to sit on the small chair to eat her bowl of meal. After she was done eating, she then proceeded to the bedroom. She saw three beds.. big, medium, and small…and decided to sleep on the small one. She wakes up after a lengthy sleep to find three bears staring at her. She got up immediately and ran away. Embarking on this adventure, Goldilocks must make crucial decisions, each choice shaping her destiny. Will she venture into the mysterious house? Which bowl of food will she choose to eat from? What chair will Goldilocks sit on? which bed will she lay on? and what should she do when the bears comes back? Only the correct choices will propel her further in this captivating tale. (Goldilocks and the Three Bears: An Interactive Fairy Tale Adventure). Eric Braun, Alex Lopez
